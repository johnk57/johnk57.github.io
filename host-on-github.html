To host your website for free on github:


1) Have your parents create an account for you at: https://github.com/


2) When you're logged in, click here to create a new repository: https://github.com/new


3) You have to name the repository username.github.io, where username is your username on github.


4) Don't worry if it takes you to a page with instructions for cloning the repository. Instead, once it's created, go to the repository which will be at: https://github.com/username/username.github.io

  • Bookmark that page, because that's where to upload your website files.


5) Click the "Add file" dropdown menu and choose "Upload files" and upload your .html file. You have to scroll down and click "Commit changes" to complete the upload.
  • If your file is named "index.html" then it will automatically load whenever anyone visits your site, which will be at: https://username.github.io/
  • If your file name is named anything else, then you can view it online at https://username.github.io/filename.html
  • You'll also have to upload any images used in your website.
  • It may take a few minutes until your website is viewable.
